Hi! I'm Charlie Walters, a Neuroscience PhD candidate at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, Maryland. My current research in the lab of Dr. Marshall Hussain Shuler is aimed towards understanding how time is represented in the brain in order to make decisions about rewarding options that are displaced in time. These time-factoring decisions are essential for evolutionary fitness in animals, where time must be allocated efficiently when searching the environment for food or a mate.
